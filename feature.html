Features

Fast & Easy Inventory upload process

Adding your stock, and keeping track of them while maintaining absolute control over their sales cycle and services along with your customers is made extremely easy with a Dealer Management System from Dealer Plus. A complete end to end top notch software solution for car dealers made by experts of this industry.

Lead Management

Potential buyers/sellers who interact with your posts/advertisements are all valuable leads which cannot be afforded to be lost, we have an in-built solution that automatically captures all their information and presents a database of sale leads tailored for you.

Customizable website

Having your own market-place makes a big impression on potential customers and we got this feature specially made for that, with dozens of templates and “click-drag-drop” method of creation, creating and customizing your website is made simple and reliable.

Upload Hi-Res Photos & Video

Hi-resolution pictures and video presentations of your stock can be uploaded in our system so that your potential customer can easily assess your stock.

Integrated Google Analytics and custom reports

Our system has an inbuilt Google analytics report system with which you can monitor the performance of your advertisements/postings easily at any time.

Publish to Marketplaces

With just a click of a button your advertisement/post will be posted/featured in 1000’s of websites/platforms where millions of potential customers will view your stock and buy them.

Reports

Our transparent reporting system generates all the information you need to analyze your business at any given time.

Daily Dashboard

We provide an extremely user friendly dashboard where you can monitor and assess the performance of your stock 24/7.
